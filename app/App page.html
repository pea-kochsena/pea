Design Process

Step 1: Benchmark

Conducted research on competitors to gather insights and identify industry standards.

Step 2: Ideate

Created wireframes and explored various design ideas and interface options. These drafts were presented to the team for feedback and discussion, helping to refine the overall direction of the design.

Step 3: Feedback

Improved the designs by incorporating feedback from my team and analyzing user behavior within the application. Focused on minimizing content to enhance clarity and usability.

Step 4: Final Outcome

I implemented the design changes based on the feedback received and walked the team through the updated flow, ensuring everyone understands the improvements and how they enhance the user experience.

Step 5: Test

As the project is 55% complete, we have planned the scripts and tasks for the upcoming testing phase. We are preparing to conduct user testing soon to gather valuable feedback and make necessary adjustments before sending the design to the developer.
