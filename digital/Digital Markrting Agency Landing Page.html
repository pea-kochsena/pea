Digital Markrting Agency Landing Page

Role: Frontend developer and UX/UI designer

Tool: Figma,Tailwind CSS and JavaScript

Length: 1 month

Completion Status: 100% complete

Year: 2024

Overview

Design a customizable website template for Quicksite Guru, tailored for digital marketing businesses. The template will be easy to modify, with an energetic and innovative vibe, appealing to professionals seeking a modern, flexible solution.

Designed a customizable website template for Quicksite Guru, with an energetic and innovative vibe, aimed at digital marketing agencies, freelancers, and startups.

Digital Marketing Agencies: Small to mid-sized agencies seeking a modern, customizable site to showcase services and campaigns.

Freelance Marketers: Independent marketers needing a flexible, visually appealing platform for their portfolio and services

Startups & Small Businesses: New companies looking for an affordable, energetic template to build an engaging online presence quickly.

Step1 Sketch: Created initial sketches and color selections inspired by Bento UI. Presented to mentor via Figma for feedback.

Step2 Prototype: Developed an interactive prototype to test user interactions.

Step3 Development: Built the website with Tailwind CSS and JavaScript. Reviewed with mentor before finalizing for Quicksite Guru.

Design Process

Step 1: Sketch

I started by sketching the website layout, selecting a color palette that evokes energetic vibes, inspired by Bento UI. I presented the initial sketches to my mentor via Figma and gathered feedback for improvements.

Step 2: Prototype

Next, I created an interactive prototype to visualize and test user interactions before development, ensuring a smooth user experience.

Step 3: Development

I developed the website using Tailwind CSS and JavaScript, incorporating the feedback from my mentor. After final review, the website was prepared for Quicksite Guru to implement.
